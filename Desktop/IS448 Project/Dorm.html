Dormitory Parking

Welcome to the live tracking of the dormitory parking lots. Dormitory parking lots consists of street parking on Poplar Avenue next to Susquehanna and Patapsco, street parking on Hilltop Circle surrounding the dorms, lot 5, and lot 23. Parking will be generally hard to find during late night until early morning on weekdays. There is parking within a 10 minutes walking distance at lot 5. Parking is generally easiest to find in the middle of the day or at night on weekdays and saturday on weekends.

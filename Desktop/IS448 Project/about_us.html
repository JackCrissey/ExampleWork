About Us!

About iPark
iPark was developed in 2017 by a group of college students: Jack Crissey, Joonick Jun, George Nie, Paul Lee, and Rehman Uddin. The idea of iPark was created due to real life experiences of these college students. They now have designed a way where everyone (from students who live on campus to students who live off campus, teachers, visitors, etc) can gain easier access to parking at UMBC.

Our Mission
To provide the most efficient and exigent parking to all of our users while being able to increase a multitude of smiles and reduce many levels of stress.

Our Objectives

  • To always provide the most excellent and most convienent parking
  • To stabalize campus traffic flow by letting our users know about the busiest times and most convienent times for parking
  • To reduce student stress by obsoleting all the time it would take to obtain a parking spot

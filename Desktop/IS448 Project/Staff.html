Staff Parking

This is the Staff parking lots. Most staff here probably know the best places to park
and their probably isn't an issue with lots being to full. However if you are a new
instructor I would assume that the best places to park are either the parking on the loop
lots 1 and 2 if you work near the commons, Walker Ave if you work near the library and
lot 8 if you work near engineering or ITE.

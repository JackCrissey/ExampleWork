Visitor Parking

This is the visitor parking lot map and tracker. Most visitor parking lots usually aren't ever totally full, but the garage by the RAC usually fills up the fastest. If you are going to any of the academic buildings we would recommend parking in the Administration Drive Garage or Lot 9 if you need to go to the engineering or the the ITE building. If you are parking to go to a game we recommend parking in the stadium lot, Lot 5, or the commons garage.

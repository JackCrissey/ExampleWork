Top of Circle

Welcome to the live tracking for commuter parking of the top of the circle. Commuters can park on Lot 22, Lot 29, street parking on Hilltop Circle and Hilltop Road right outside the lots. Parking will be hard to find from noon until late afternoons on weekdays. There is almost always parking early in the morning, after 4pm on the weekdays and on the weekends.
